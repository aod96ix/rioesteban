Martin Nunez Arzu, CEO

Company: Rio Esteban Soccer Club Inc.

Background:

Martin Nunez Arzu, a native of Rio Esteban, Balfate Colon Honduras C.A., migrated to the United States in 1990 with the American dream of owning his own business.

Professional Journey:

  • Maintenance Worker: Upon arriving in the United States, Martin attained employment as a maintenance worker with the Local 32BJ Union.
  • Superintendent: He then progressed to becoming a superintendent, a position he held for ten years.
  • Assistant Property Manager: Currently, Martin serves as an assistant property manager with the 32BJ Union, showcasing his dedication and expertise over a span of more than 20 years.

Union Contributions:

  • As a valued member and employee of the 32BJ Union, Martin's skills and love for soccer were recognized by Union officials.
  • He proposed the idea of forming a soccer team for Union employees, leading to the establishment of a league supervised by Martin.
  • Martin continues to supervise both male and female teams sponsored by the Local 32BJ Union.

Community Engagement:

  • Martin has created a social setting through the soccer teams, bringing together team players and community residents for weekly entertainment, skill-building workshops, and trainings.
  • In 2018, Martin founded the Rio Esteban Soccer Club Inc. to provide team players and community residents with skill-building opportunities, trainings, and employment assistance.

Mission:

Martin's vision for the Rio Esteban Soccer Club Inc. is to foster a sense of community, enhance skills, and provide valuable opportunities for personal and professional growth.
